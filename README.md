## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes a Walmart sales dataset (10,000+ records) to understand customer behavior, sales trends, and business performance.
Using SQL, I solved multiple business problems that help in data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across different branches and cities  
- Understand customer preferences and behavior  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Evaluate payment methods and transaction trends  
- Generate actionable business insights  

---

## 🧠 Business Questions Solved

1. What are the different payment methods and how many transactions/items were sold with each?
2. Which category received the highest average rating in each branch?
3. What is the busiest day of the week for each branch?
4. What are the average, minimum, and maximum ratings per category in each city?
5. What is the total profit for each category (ranked)?
6. What is the most frequently used payment method in each branch?
7. How many transactions occur in each shift (Morning, Afternoon, Evening)?
8. What is the average unit price per category and its correlation with ratings?
9. How does customer rating vary by time of day?

---

## 📁 Dataset

- Source: Walmart Sales Dataset (Kaggle)
- Records: 10,000+
- Features include:
  - Invoice ID
  - Branch & City
  - Product Category
  - Unit Price & Quantity
  - Payment Method
  - Rating
  - Profit Margin
  - Date & Time
